Privacy Policy for Guitar Mastery Hub

Last Updated: December 2025

Introduction

Guitar Mastery Hub ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection

What Data We Collect

Guitar Mastery Hub collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Reading Progress
    • Last position in each chapter
    • Completion status of chapters
    • Timestamp of last update
  • User Preferences
    • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
    • Font size preference
    • Display settings
  • Bookmarks
    • Chapters or sections you bookmark for quick access
  • Notes
    • Personal notes you create within chapters

What Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Browsing history or website data
  • Location information
  • Device information
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Any data outside of the extension's local storage

Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All data is stored exclusively on your device using Chrome’s local storage API (chrome.storage.local):

  • Data never leaves your computer
  • Data is not transmitted to any external servers
  • Data is not shared with third parties
  • Data is not accessible to other extensions or websites

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until:

  • You uninstall the extension
  • You manually clear Chrome's extension storage
  • You use Chrome's "Clear browsing data" feature

Data Usage

How We Use Your Data

  • Enhance Your Experience
    • Remember reading position
    • Save preferences
    • Store bookmarks
    • Preserve notes

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to third parties.

Permissions Explained

  • Storage: Store preferences and progress locally
  • Downloads: Allow users to download the complete guide as a PDF
  • SidePanel: Display UI in Chrome side panel

Security

  • All data stored locally using secure APIs
  • No external servers used
  • No login or authentication required

Your Control

  • Uninstall extension to delete all data
  • Clear data via Chrome settings

Children's Privacy

This extension is suitable for all ages. No personal data is collected.

## Privacy Policy for Guitar Mastery Hub

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

Guitar Mastery Hub ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Data Collection

### What Data We Collect

Guitar Mastery Hub collects and stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

1. **Reading Progress**
   - Last position in each chapter
   - Completion status of chapters
   - Timestamp of last update

2. **User Preferences**
   - Theme preference (light/dark mode)
   - Font size preference
   - Display settings

3. **Bookmarks**
   - Chapters or sections you bookmark for quick access

4. **Notes**
   - Personal notes you create within chapters

5. **License Status** (if applicable)
   - Premium access status
   - Purchase verification status
   - Test mode status (development only)

### What Data We Do NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect, transmit, or store:
- Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- Browsing history or website data
- Location information
- Device information
- Analytics or tracking data
- Any data outside of the extension's local storage

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only

All data collected by Guitar Mastery Hub is stored **exclusively on your device** using Chrome's local storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). This means:

- ✅ Data never leaves your computer
- ✅ Data is not transmitted to any external servers
- ✅ Data is not shared with third parties
- ✅ Data is not accessible to other extensions or websites

### Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until:
- You uninstall the extension (data is automatically deleted)
- You manually clear Chrome's extension storage
- You use Chrome's "Clear browsing data" feature for extension data

## Data Usage

### How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used **solely** to:

1. **Enhance Your Experience**
   - Remember your reading position to resume where you left off
   - Save your theme and font preferences
   - Store your bookmarks for quick access
   - Preserve your personal notes

2. **Provide Premium Features**
   - Verify premium access status
   - Enable PDF download functionality (premium users only)

### Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.** All data remains on your device.

## Third-Party Services

### Buy Me a Coffee

When you choose to upgrade to premium access:
- The extension opens the Buy Me a Coffee website in a new tab
- Any purchase is handled entirely by Buy Me a Coffee
- We do not receive or store payment information
- We do not have access to your Buy Me a Coffee account details
- Purchase verification is handled through Buy Me a Coffee's platform

**Buy Me a Coffee Privacy Policy:** https://www.buymeacoffee.com/privacy

## Permissions Explained

### Storage Permission
**Purpose:** Store your preferences, progress, bookmarks, and notes locally on your device.
**Data:** Only stored locally, never transmitted.

### Downloads Permission
**Purpose:** Allow premium users to download the complete guide as a PDF.
**Usage:** Only activated when you explicitly click the "Download PDF" button.

### SidePanel Permission
**Purpose:** Display the learning interface in Chrome's side panel.
**Data:** No data collection related to this permission.

### Host Permissions (buymeacoffee.com)
**Purpose:** Open the Buy Me a Coffee page when you choose to upgrade.
**Usage:** Only used when you click the "Upgrade" button. No data is transmitted.

## Security

### Data Security

- All data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage API
- No data is transmitted over the internet (except when opening Buy Me a Coffee)
- No external servers or databases are used
- No authentication or login is required

### Your Control

You have complete control over your data:
- Clear all data by uninstalling the extension
- Clear specific data using Chrome's storage management
- Export is not currently available (data is only stored locally)

## Children's Privacy

Guitar Mastery Hub is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect personal information at all, this is not a concern.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

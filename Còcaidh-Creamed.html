Privacy Policy - Còcaidh Creamed

Last updated: March 2026

Summary

Còcaidh Creamed does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

This extension collects no data. It does not:

  • Track browsing history
  • Collect or transmit the URLs you visit
  • Store identifiers or usage statistics
  • Connect to external servers or APIs
  • Use analytics or crash reporting services

Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions solely to perform its stated function:

Permission Why it is needed
cookies Read and delete cookies for the current site.
storage Save extension preferences locally on your device only.
activeTab Identify the current tab's URL.
scripting Clear localStorage, sessionStorage, and optionally IndexedDB in the current tab.
contextMenus Add a right-click menu option.
host_permissions (http/https) Required by Chrome to access and delete cookies across sites.

Local Storage Only

The only data stored by this extension is your own settings (for example, whether auto-reload is enabled). This is saved in Chrome's storage.local API, stays on your device, and is never transmitted.
